## Analysis

Josiah (or Josias or Joshaiah or Iosias) conceived Jeconiah (or Jehoiachin or Jechoniah or Coniah or Jechonias or Jakim or Iakim) and his brothers at a time of exile to babylon.

So, from this we can infer the following:

- Josiah --> Jeconiah & brothers
