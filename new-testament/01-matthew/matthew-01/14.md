## Analysis

Azor conceived Zadok (or Sadoc or Sadok or Zadoq), who conceived Akim (or Achim or Achen or Achin), who conceived Elihud (or Eliud).

So, from this we can infer the following:

- Azor --> Zadok --> Akim --> Elihud
