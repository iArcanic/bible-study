## Analysis

Salmon and Rahab conceived Boaz (or Boos or Booz), who convieved Obed with Ruth (or Rath), and Obed fathered from Jesse.

So, from this we can infer the following:

- Salmon & Rahab --> Boaz
- Boaz & Ruth --> Obed --> Jesse
